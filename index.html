About This Work

This is a visual and musical work of Hermann Hesse’s Siddhartha, designed with minimalism and a golden-rose theme. The music reflects Siddhartha’s journey described in the story, while the melody and the chord represent his disciplined daily life, his desires, and wisdom.

The Golden-rose tones helps the user to feel warm and comfortable atmosphere while appreciate the work.

AI tools assisted in the creative process, especially website creation and music generation. However, literature interpretation and composition of the page is fully constructed by the creator, 1304 Su-Hyun Kim.

Literary Insight

The layout, colour, and the structure of the cards visually represents the theme of ‘Siddhartha.’ Its simple whitespace symbolizes the journey to attain wisdom. Gold emphasizes his inner growth and transcendence, while rose suggests his inner comfort.

AI tools supported idea generation and assisted in creating the website and music. However, the interpretation and composition of the page is fully constructed by the creator, 1304 Su-Hyun Kim.
